MECO is directed by the University of Missouri - Columbia, Campus Facilities
Planning and Design Construction Division each year to study, evaluate, and
recommend solutions for parking lot areas located throughout the campus and in
various stages of need for maintenance repair and/or reconstruction. Pavements
are evaluated and various options for repair or rebuild are considered. Opinions of
probable project costs are prepared, reviewed by the University, and the option
that both upgrades the existing lot and fits within the budget constraints is
determined for each of the targeted parking lots. 
Options considered for each included seal coats, chip and seal surfacing, milling of
existing pavements and placing new asphalt overlays and/or chip and seal
surfacing, as well as concrete pavements replaced. The extent of the existing state
of deterioration controlled the extent of repair or reconstruction. Plans and
specifications are prepared and provided to the University for the university's use in
the bidding of the projects, provided project administration (awarding, change
orders, etc.) and generally we conduct construction observation services for the
project.
